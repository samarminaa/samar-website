## Overview

San Diego offers perfect weather, world-class beaches, and a thriving food scene. This guide covers a long weekend exploring America's Finest City.

## Neighborhoods

- **North Park**: Hip bars, coffee shops, and vintage stores
- **La Jolla**: Stunning coves and seals on the beach
- **Old Town**: Historic sites and Mexican food
- **Gaslamp Quarter**: Downtown dining and nightlife

## Beaches

La Jolla Cove for snorkeling, Pacific Beach for people-watching, Coronado for a classic California beach day.
