## Overview

Austin is Texas' cultural capitalâ€”a city of live music, breakfast tacos, and outdoor swimming holes. This guide covers the essentials for a long weekend.

## Music

Catch live music any night of the week on 6th Street or South Congress. Check the calendar at venues like Mohawk, Stubb's, and Continental Club.

## Food

- Breakfast tacos at Veracruz All Natural
- BBQ at Franklin (get there early) or la Barbecue
- Tex-Mex at Matt's El Rancho

## Outdoors

Barton Springs Pool offers a refreshing escape from the Texas heat. The greenbelt has miles of hiking trails within city limits.
