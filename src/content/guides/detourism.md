## About This Guide

Detourism is a philosophy of travel that prioritizes meaningful experiences over tourist checkboxes. This ebook explores how to travel more thoughtfully, sustainably, and authentically.

## What's Inside

- Principles of slow travel
- How to support local economies
- Reducing your environmental impact
- Finding off-the-beaten-path experiences
- Connecting with local communities
