## Overview

Costa Rica packs incredible biodiversity into a small country. This guide covers a two-week trip through cloud forests, volcanoes, and both coasts.

## Regions

- **Arenal**: Hot springs and volcano views
- **Monteverde**: Cloud forest hikes and wildlife
- **Manuel Antonio**: Beaches and national park
- **Caribbean Coast**: Laid-back vibes in Puerto Viejo
