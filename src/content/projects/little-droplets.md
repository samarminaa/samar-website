## Overview

Little Droplets is a children's book about the water cycle, designed to make science accessible and engaging for young readers. The project involved creating all illustrations and designing the book layout.

## Visual Style

The illustrations use a soft, watercolor-inspired digital style with gentle colors and friendly character designs. Each spread was designed to guide the reader's eye through the story while leaving room for imagination.

## Publication

The book is available through independent bookstores and online retailers.
