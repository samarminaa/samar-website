## Overview

Apolo is a music discovery app that helps users find new artists based on their listening habits and mood. The goal was to create an experience that feels effortless and intuitive, making music discovery feel like a natural extension of listening.

## Design Process

We conducted extensive user research to understand how people discover new music today. The insights led us to focus on context-aware recommendationsâ€”suggesting music based on time of day, activity, and emotional state.

## Key Features

- **Mood-based discovery**: Swipe through mood cards to find music that matches how you're feeling
- **Collaborative playlists**: Create and share playlists with friends in real-time
- **Artist deep dives**: Explore an artist's full catalog with curated entry points
