# Hi, I'm Samar.

I'm a data and design strategist based in Brooklyn, NY.

You can currently find me at [Brooklyn Data](https://brooklyndata.com), but more importantly, you can find me disassembling watches, bottling homemade cold brew, writing travel guides, playing guitar, or balling at the Y.

# A Brief History

- **August 2017 — Georgetown University**
  Majored in Operations and Information Management. Became a Global Business Fellow.

- **June 2018 — Shut Up and Go**
  Became a writer at a small travel company. Wrote an ebook on sustainable travel.

- **January 2019 — ESADE**
  Spent a summer abroad at ESADE in Barcelona on a photojournalism scholarship.

- **June 2019 — McDonough Case Team**
  Founded the Global Case Team, traveling to Canada and Norway to compete in business case competitions.

- **October 2019 — Agora Partnerships**
  Interned in Mexico City at an entrepreneurship network dedicated to social impact.

- **March 2020 — Fahrenheit 212**
  Interned at an innovation studio in NYC. Developed a growth strategy for OREO.

- **June 2020 — Pandemic**
  Went back home. Built a smart mirror.

- **December 2020 — Aspen Institute**
  Interned at the Aspen Network of Development Entrepreneurs (ANDE).

- **January 2021 — Graduated!**
  Finished up Georgetown a semester early, graduating Magna Cum Laude.

- **August 2021 — Body Coffee**
  After spending six weeks in Central America, started a cold brew company with my partner. Sold at Pop Up Grocer.

- **February 2023 — frog design**
  Moved to NYC and started my career at frog design as a strategy analyst. After a RIF, moved to Capgemini, frog's parent company.

- **July 2024 — 1:46 Watch Repair**
  Began a watchmaking apprenticeship with 1:46 in Williamsburg. Built their website and new brand.

- **October 2024 — Brooklyn Data**
  Became a Manager at Brooklyn Data, a data & analytics consultancy, helping small teams unlock the power of their data.
